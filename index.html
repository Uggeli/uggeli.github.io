Featured Projects

CTN (Contingent Task Network) Planning Algorithm

Deterministic, from-scratch planning system for real-time AI and simulation.
Built as an A*/GOAP replacement—100% custom architecture.

  • Hierarchical task decomposition, robust cycle handling, universal “bridge” actions.
  • Sub-millisecond plan times for thousands of agents; lets worlds scale and remain responsive.
  • Fits event-driven, ECS-style engines and persistent simulations.

WebPeli – Simulation Sandbox Engine

Systemic, agent-driven sandbox with emergent gameplay, moddable systems, and persistent worlds.

  • Unified rules for AI & player; ECS-style struct entity system, chunked spatial storage.
  • Highly modular, event-driven, and scalable (WebGL/WebGPU client planned).
  • AI with needs, memory, emotion, and group dynamics.

Proteus – Research Neural Network Platform

Modular neuroevolution and RL system, 100% handcrafted in C#—no external ML libraries used.
Built from scratch for maximum flexibility, understanding, and fun.

  • SIMD batch computation, multi-threaded, GPU accelerated, advanced serialization.
  • Meta-learning, n-way crossover, hierarchical embeddings (Poincaré disk).
  • Real-time dashboards and robust test coverage.

GladiatorArena

Simulation-driven gladiator management with RPG and physiological depth.

  • Body modeled as a dynamic graph (blood, nerves, injuries, magic).
  • Magic tightly integrated with physiology; resource management is gameplay core.
  • Designed for extensibility and procedural content.
